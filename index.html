Is IpponBoard free?

The Scoreboard app is completely free ‚Äî no account, no in-app purchases. The Club Management platform has a free Starter plan for up to 10 members, with paid plans for larger clubs.

What martial arts does IpponBoard support?

The Scoreboard follows Olympic judo rules. The Club Management platform works for any martial art with belt or grade systems ‚Äî judo, BJJ, karate, taekwondo, aikido, and more.

Do I need separate apps for scoring and club management?

They're independent products. The Scoreboard is a standalone desktop/iPad app. Club Management is a web-based platform. Use either or both ‚Äî they don't require each other.

No. The Scoreboard is free to download. Club Management offers a 30-day free trial with no credit card required.
